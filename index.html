ShopCPR Resources

Basic Life Support (BLS) Medical (Initial course)

This course is designed for healthcare professionals and other medical personnel to promptly recognize several life-threatening emergencies, give high-quality CPR, deliver appropriate ventilations and provide early use of an AED.

Basic Life Support (BLS) Medical (Renewal course)

This course is designed for healthcare professionals and other medical personnel to promptly recognize several life-threatening emergencies, give high-quality CPR, deliver appropriate ventilations and provide early use of an AED.

Heartsaver CPR AED

This course covers high-quality CPR and how to use an automated external defibrillator (AED). This course is designed for anyone with little or no medical training who have a duty to respond to a cardiac emergency in a safe, timely, and effective manner due to job responsibilities.

Heartsaver First Aid

This course covers basic first aid situations. This course is designed for anyone with little or no medical training who have a duty to respond to a first aid situation in a safe and timely manner due to job responsibilities.

Heartsaver First Aid CPR AED

This course covers high-quality CPR and how to use and automated external defibrillator (AED) and how to respond to first aid situations. This course is designed for anyone with little or no medical training to provide both first aid and CPR with the use an automated external defibrillator (AED) in a safe, timely, and effective manner due to job responsibilities.

Friends & Family

Family & Friends CPR is for people who want to learn CPR but do not need a CPR course completion card to meet a job requirement. This course is ideal for community groups, new parents, grandparents, babysitters, and others interested in learning how to save a life.
