About Kelli

Owner and CPR Instructor Kelli has been a CPR Instructor for over 16 years. She is a wife and a mother to two wonderful sons, Lawrence and William.

How Give-A-Hand CPR got its start:

Kelli has been an instructor since 2009, only teaching in her spare time.
After her son, William, was born, he was diagnosed with Down syndrome. Kelli decided to leave the healthcare eld after 15 years to pursue a teaching career in Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) full time. This allows her more exibility to be with her son, William and have a career and own and operate her business.
Give-A-Hand CPR was established in 2011. As an instructor, Kelli is dedicated to making sure her students get the very best training and have a full understanding of all aspects of their training. Give-A-Hand CPR o ers classes for both the medical professionals & healthcare providers, daycare providers, teachers, students, and friends and family classes.
